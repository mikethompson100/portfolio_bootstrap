Modal title

With data-bs-backdrop="static" clicking on the backdrop will not close this modal.

Modal title

With data-bs-keyboard="false" the Escape key will not close this modal. Without having data-bs-backdrop="static", clicking on the backdrop will also close this modal.

Modal title

Because Bootstrap modals are on div tags, they are not naturally focusable like with an anchor or button. By having tabindex="-1" we can have it focusable but it won't interrupt the normal tabbing order of the page. When the modal is opened, Bootstrap uses focus() to give the modal focus.
